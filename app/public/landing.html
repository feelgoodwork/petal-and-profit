How it works

Four steps. Most of the work is on our side.

No software to install. No POS to change. No card on file. You upload, we process, you keep the report.

01

You apply

Fill out the form below. We confirm fit and reach back within one business day.

02

You upload

12 months of supplier invoices and your recipe library. PDFs, scans, or phone photos. About 30 minutes of your time on a private upload link.

03

We process

We extract every line item, match it to your recipes, layer in market benchmarks, and build your report. Two to four days.

04

You get the report

A 30-minute walk-through call and a written report you keep. The only thing we ask back is your feedback on what was useful.

The honest part

Why we're doing this for free.

We built and tested Petal & Profit on one shop's data. 250 invoices, 657 recipes, three years of sales. The system works.

One shop is not enough.

Before we charge anyone for this, we want to see it run on ten shops with ten different vendor mixes, recipe styles, and price points. Your data tells us where the system is right and where it isn't. You get a report we'd otherwise sell. No payment changes hands. No commitment to buy anything later.

Who qualifies

We're looking for 10 independent retail flower shops.

If you've been running the shop for a year or more, you have invoices and recipes in some form, and you can give us 60 minutes total of your time, you're a fit. Format does not matter. Mess does not matter.

You're a fit if
  • You have at least 12 months of supplier invoices in any format
  • You have a recipe library or design book, even paper
  • You can be on a 30-minute walk-through when the report is ready
  • You're willing to tell us what was useful and what wasn't
We're not the right fit for
  • Wedding-only or event-only studios
  • Pop-up businesses without a steady weekly inventory
  • Shops without 12 months of records
